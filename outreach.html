Outreach & Teaching

Outreach

Our research group is very active on instagram and twitter! We usually post about graduate student life and the type of research we do. We hope to show our followers the many different faces of people in physics.

For the past year I have also been a co-host of the STEMcoding astronomy YouTube series, where we explain physics and astronomy concepts through coding.

STEMcoding


Teaching

During my time as a graduate student I have been a Teaching Assistant for introductory astronomy and introductory physics recitations/labs, teaching both calculus-based and algebra-based electromagnetism. For these classes I worked out problems during recitation, led lab sessions, graded, and held office hours.
